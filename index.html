OUR SERVICES

Doggy Day-Care

Our Dog Daycare is tailor-made to give your dog the best experience together with some socialization training. Every session is supervised by our trained Paw Patrons and every dog is taken care of accordingly.

Dog Boarding

Every pet in our care will experience comfort and security because that is our first priority. We provide a la carte services to supplement our regular boarding package because no dog is exactly like yours, allowing for a completely personalized stay.

Dog Training

We design dog training regimens that are simple to understand and practical to apply on a regular basis. You can discover the benefits of having a fresh relationship with your dog by learning how to speak to it properly and efficiently.
